## Incident & fix (recorded for future agents)
- **Dev-db migration state glitch**: the first `migrate` of the combined
  pis+matching batch failed on matching.0003 with "pis_photoprobe does not
  exist" — django_migrations had pis.0001 recorded although its DDL never
  landed. Fixed by deleting the pis.0001 row (guarded by
  to_regclass IS NULL) and re-running migrate; both applied cleanly.
  If a multi-app migrate crashes mid-run on Windows, verify
  django_migrations vs actual tables before retrying.
